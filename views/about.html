What is image colorization?

Color is a crucial part in photos. Back in the era of black and white photos, every image had to stand on its own, in order for them to relive, we proposed an Image Colourization model which is based on Artificial Intelligence. A fully automatic approach which produces vibrant and realistic colorizations using Deep Learning techniques and methods with a user intervention
